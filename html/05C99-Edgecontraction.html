edge-contraction


Given an edge x⁢y of a graph G, the graph G/x⁢y is obtained from G by contracting the edge x⁢y; that is, to get G/x⁢y we identify the vertices x and y and remove all loops and duplicate edges. A graph G′ obtained by a sequence of edge-contractions is said to be a contraction of G.
