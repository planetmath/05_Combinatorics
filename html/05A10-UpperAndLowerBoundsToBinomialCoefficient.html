upper and lower bounds to binomial coefficient


Given two integers n,k>0 such that k≤n, we have the following inequalities for the binomial coefficient (nk):

(nk) ≤ nkk!
(nk) ≤ (n⋅ek)k
(nk) ≥ (nk)k

Here e is the base of natural logarithms. Also, for large n, (nk)≈nkk!.
